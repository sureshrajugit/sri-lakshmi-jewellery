Who We Are ?

At Lakshmi Jewellers, we are more than just a jewellery store — we are custodians of tradition and crafters of timeless beauty. Based in Alangulam, our journey began with a simple promise: to offer pure gold, exceptional designs, and heartfelt service. Every ornament we create reflects our devotion to culture, craftsmanship, and customer trust.

Who We Do ?

We design and deliver exquisite gold and silver jewellery for every occasion — from weddings and festivals to daily elegance. Our collection includes:

  • Bridal Sets
  • Temple Jewellery
  • Modern Minimalist Designs
  • Custom-made Ornaments

With two showrooms in Alangulam, we bring quality and artistry closer to you — offering a seamless shopping experience with genuine pricing and purity you can trust.
